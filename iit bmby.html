30 Years of Evolution

Techfest IIT Bombay began as a spark of curiosity in 1993 and has since grown into Asia's largest science and technology festival, drawing minds from across 56 nations.

The 30th edition — NEXUS — explores the blurring boundary between biological intelligence and machine architecture. Where does the human end and the cyborg begin?

Three days. 100+ events. 850+ teams. The upgrade is inevitable.
